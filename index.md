## About Walk Now
Walk Now is a fitness rewards app that tracks your daily steps and rewards you with real money when you reach your walking goals. Stay active, earn rewards, withdraw your earnings, and connect with friends!

## App Features
- **Step Tracking**: Automatically counts your daily steps  
- **Daily Rewards**: Earn financial rewards by reaching your daily walk limit  
- **Rewards Wheel**: Spin the wheel to win random bonus rewards  
- **Withdraw Earnings**: Cash out your rewards directly to your account  
- **Friends System**: Connect and interact with other users 
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on both iOS and Android

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does step tracking work?
The app uses your device's built-in sensors to accurately track your steps throughout the day. Make sure to grant the necessary permissions for accurate tracking.

### How do I earn rewards?
Simply walk! When you reach your daily step goal, you'll automatically earn rewards that can be used within the app or transferred to other users.

### What is the rewards wheel?
The rewards wheel allows you to spin and earn random extra rewards such as coins, bonuses, or special prizes.

### How do I transfer money to other users?
Navigate to the transfer section in the app, enter the recipient's details, and confirm the amount you wish to send.

### How to Report a Bug?
Please email us at quriv@support with:

- Description of the issue  
- Steps to reproduce the problem  
- Screenshots if possible  

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We take your privacy seriously. All personal and financial data is encrypted and stored securely.

## Technical Support

### App not tracking steps?
1. Check that motion permissions are enabled  
2. Ensure the app is running in the background  
3. Restart your device  
4. Update to the latest app version  

### Rewards not appearing?
Please allow up to 24 hours for rewards to be processed. If the issue persists, contact support.

### Can't transfer money?
Verify that:  
- You have sufficient balance  
- The recipient's information is correct  
- Your account is in good standing
